Simple Text Classifier

This application allows you to upload your labeled training data and train a text classification model. After model training, you will be presented with relevant model training performance metrics. You will also be able to use the model to classify arbitrary text that you enter in a form. Under the hood, the application uses a multinomial Naive Bayes classifier.

The training data needs to be formatted as a tab delimited text file where both label_i and text_i are arbitrary text like this:

label_1 \t text_1 \n
label_2 \t text_2 \n
â€¦
label_n \t text_3 \n

Upload the training data

Only click "Train Model" once. Processing could take a long time on a large dataset. Small data sets (about 1000 lines of text such as the restaurant review sentiment analysis sample data) take a few seconds to process. Larger data sets (tens of thousands of lines of text such as the spooky author classification sample data) take a few minutes to process.
